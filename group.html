Group Activities

Our experienced and highly trained support workers provide the highest quality of care to both clients and their families. Our goal is to help individuals and their loved ones to live happy and fulfilling lives. We celebrate life by celebrating every milestone​. We are here to help you continue enjoying your hobbies to stay connected with your community or make new friends.


The Arts

Our community hubs run a range of activities including arts and crafts, performing arts and music all tailored to develop our clients creative skills. Our clients can develop their skills as an artists and take part in art exhibitions across Perth.

Sports and recreation

Group classes such as joining the gym or try out a new sport. We have connection with local inclusive and abilities sporting clubs

Independent living skills

Learn about shopping, cooking, budgeting, paying bills, gardening, using public transports and many more

Contact Us

Let's get united

You can contact us through this contact form by providing your details in order to be connected with us. You are all free to send us messages mentioning your problems and yeah we're always there for you.
